Our Story

Family & Farm

A Homestead
Built on Love

Wild Hearts Homestead is a small family homestead in Northern Utah focused on creating fresh artisan sourdough, homemade baked goods, and farm-fresh eggs for our local community.

Everything is made with care in small batches and shared through our self-serve farm stand. We believe food made with love tastes better — and we're here to prove it every single weekend.
